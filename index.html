FAQ: Password Security

A strong password is at least 12 characters long and includes a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
Password cracking is the process of attempting to guess or decrypt a password to gain unauthorized access to a system or account. Using strong and complex passwords helps protect against this.
It's a good practice to change your passwords periodically, at least every 3 to 6 months, or sooner if you suspect that your password may have been compromised.
